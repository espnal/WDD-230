Target Audience:

People mostly between 22 to 60 with a good command of technology and with the intention of acquiring a property directly with the owner of the property, choosing from a variety of options based on the qualities and the price they are looking for.

Junior Meza

Meza junior is a 30 year old young man with less than 5 years working in his professional area after graduating from college, currently Junior lives renting an apartment with his wife and the two have thought of investing in a small property to remodel and later receive passive income.

Gabi Tikan

Gabi Tikan, a 39 year old single mother who works while leaving her children in the care of a nanny, earns very well but realizes she needs to spend more time with her children before it is too late.... She decides to look into investment opportunities with a relatively high budget and to invest in a medium term business and have more freedom later on, Gabi has the task of looking for a place that suits her budget and needs.
